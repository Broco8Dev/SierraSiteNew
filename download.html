How to Direcly Sideload Sierra

Step 1: When prompted, press 'Install'

You will be prompted to install Sierra. Accept it.

Step 2: Verify it

Go into Settings, General, VPN & Device Management, find the Sierra Certificate then verify it.
If on iOS 18, there will be additional steps on-screen. For iOS 17-, simply verify then enjoy Sierra!

How to Install Sierra's iPA

Step 1: Download the iPA

Download the .iPA file to your files.

Step 2: Pick a Sideloading app.

Pick an app for Sideloading Sierra. Popular options include ESign, Scarlet, AltStore, etc.

Step 3: Import your file

This step will vary depending on your app, but a common method is using the Share Sheet pn the iPA in the files app and then selecting your Sideloading app.
